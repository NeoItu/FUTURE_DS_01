E-commerce Sales Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

1. Project Overview
This project is about analyzing SuperStore sales data to understand how the business is performing. I used Power BI to create a dashboard and find useful insights from the data.

2. Project Objectives

. To understand how sales are changing over time

. To check which regions are performing well

. To compare different product categories

. To find problems and areas to improve

. To give simple recommendations

3. Key Insights

3.1 Overall Sales Performance
   
. Sales increased from 2014 to 2017.

. The number of products sold also increased over time.

. Discounts also increased during this period.

This shows that sales are growing, but maybe discounts are helping to increase sales.

3.2 Regional Performance

. West region has the highest sales.

. East is the second highest.

. Central is in the middle.

. South has the lowest sales.

This means South region is not doing well compared to others.

3.3 Category Performance

. Office Supplies has the highest sales.

. Technology has lower sales but the highest profit.

. Furniture has okay sales but very low profit.

This shows that not all sales bring good profit.

3.4 Furniture Category Problem

. Furniture is not making much profit.

. Even though it sells, it does not earn much money.

This could be because of high costs or too many discounts.

3.5 Top Selling Products
. One product (Canon imageCLASS) sells much more than others.

This means the business depends a lot on one product, which can be risky.

3.6 Discount Trend

. Discounts increased over time.

. Sales also increased with discounts.

This shows discounts may be helping sales, but they can reduce profit.

4. Recommendations
   
. Try to reduce discounts a little and check if sales stay the same

. Improve Furniture category or remove low-performing products

. Focus more on Technology products because they give high profit

. Try to improve sales in South region

. Promote more products so the business does not depend on one product

5. Conclusion
   
. Sales are increasing, which is good

. Discounts are also increasing, which may reduce profit

. Technology is the best category for profit

. Furniture is not performing well

. South region needs improvement

The business should focus more on profit, not just sales.
